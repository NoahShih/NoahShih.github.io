Playmaking

Ben Simmons is also good at making plays for his teammates. Ben Simmons’ scoring ability also helps his playmaking by forcing defenders to help and leave their man which leads to an easy pass. Ben Simmons playmaking and speed allows him to push the ball up the court and start fast breaks. He can even go coast to coast with his amazing handles. Ben Simmons is also very unselfish because he is willing to give up his shot for a better shot from a different teammate. Ben Simmons’ passing ability is good due to his court vision and perfect on target passes.
